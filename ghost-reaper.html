Ghost Reaper

This is Ghost Reaper. I started this drawing assignment in illustrator when I was waiting for my friend in the library and it was an extra work that I did for our course and it turned out to be my favourite vector drawing that I have ever done. It did not take long and it really challenged me and pushed me to learn more about layers and orientation of them in illustrator.
